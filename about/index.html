Info

I’m a designer based in Washington, D.C. with interests in UI/UX design, graphic design, branding, and fashion. I studied computer science in college and over time began to realize that I cared a lot more about why certain products are great and what problems they are solving rather than how they are made, which led me to shift my focus to design.

I love creating work with elegant and compelling visuals, and sometimes making things just for fun without any other real purpose. However, when it comes to creating for a user, I’m a big believer in the idea that a great design means nothing and is incomplete until somebody is using it.

Nowadays, in my free time, you can probably find me sketching, listening to music, or watching a movie.
